Bio

I'm a software engineer with 9 years of experience building scalable, reliable systems. I currently work at Amazon Web Services (AWS) on the Amazon ECS team, where I own and lead development of ECS Service Connect—a feature that simplifies service discovery and communication in containerized applications.

My work centers around containers, with a strong focus on container orchestration and application networking. I'm passionate about solving hard distributed systems problems and designing tools that make infrastructure seamless for developers.

My expertise spans API and database design, large-scale distributed architectures, and building high availability systems that power production workloads.
